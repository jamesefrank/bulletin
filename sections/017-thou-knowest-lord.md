Thou knowest, Lord,
the secrets of our hearts;
shut not thy merciful ears
unto our prayers;
but spare us, Lord most holy,
O God most mighty.

O holy and most merciful Savior,
thou most worthy Judge eternal,
suffer us not, at our last hour,
for any pains of death,
to fall from thee.
Amen.
